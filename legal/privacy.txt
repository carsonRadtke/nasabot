Privacy Policy for nasabot

Effective Date: Sep 18, 2024

1. Introduction
nasabot ("we", "our", "us") is a Discord bot that queries api.nasa.gov for various
space-related information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that
your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

2. Information We Collect
nasabot does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The bot only
processes commands and queries to provide space-related information from api.nasa.gov.

3. Use of Information
Since nasabot does not collect any personal data, there is no information to use or
share. All interactions with the bot are ephemeral and are not stored or logged.

4. Third-Party Services
nasabot interacts with the NASA API (api.nasa.gov) to retrieve space-related
information. Please refer to NASA's privacy policy for more information on how they
handle data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by
posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy
Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at
https://github.com/carsonRadtke/nasabot/issues.

By using nasabot, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
